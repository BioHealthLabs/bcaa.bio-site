How to choose the right BCAA supplement

Picking the right BCAA formula depends on your training goals and intensity. Check out the different ratios and ingredients before buying.

  • 2:1:1 vs 4:1:1: If you're focused on recovery, 2:1:1 is a solid all-rounder. For endurance, 4:1:1 is an excellent choice.
  • Hydration: Make sure your BCAA includes electrolytes if you're training in hot climates or doing long workouts.
  • Price-per-serving: Bulk powders may offer more servings per container for a better deal.

BCAA FAQ

What is BCAA and why do I need it?

BCAAs (Branched-Chain Amino Acids) are essential amino acids that help muscle recovery and prevent muscle breakdown during exercise.

How much BCAA should I take?

Aim for 5-10g of BCAAs per serving, depending on your intensity and goals. The 2:1:1 ratio is a popular choice for optimal results.

Can I take BCAA every day?

Yes, BCAAs are safe to consume daily, especially if you are training intensely or need extra recovery support.
